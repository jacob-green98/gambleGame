The point of the game is get the highest score without rolling double

Game Rules

  • Click the "Roll Dice" button to start the game, then keep clicking the blue "ok" button to continue rolling
  • The number on the dice you roll gets added to your score
  • If you roll doubles, the game immideialty ends and you're left with a zero score
  • You can stop the game anytime and lock in your score by hitting the "stop" button
